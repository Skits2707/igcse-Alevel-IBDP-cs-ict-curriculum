Why Me — Connor Scanlan

Experienced Computer Science educator with a proven track record building examination-ready curricula for international schools across Asia.

I specialise in designing coherent K–12 CS & ICT pathways that align with Cambridge Assessment, IB Diploma Programme, and international best practice. Every document in this proposal has been crafted to meet the exacting standards of Victoria Park Academy Shenzhen.

From IGCSE Computer Science (0478) through A-Level and IB DP, to a full ICT programme beginning in Grade 2, this curriculum offers VPA students a seamless progression — whether they pursue CS at university or apply digital literacy across any discipline.

Programme Overview

Four interconnected pathways designed for Victoria Park Academy Shenzhen, each with detailed schemes of work, assessment packs, and supporting resources.

💻
Cambridge IGCSE 0478

IGCSE Computer Science

Two-year programme covering data representation, hardware, networks, programming fundamentals, and problem solving. Full assessment packs and revision materials included.

20 documents
🎓
Cambridge A-Level

A-Level Computer Science

AS and A2 levels with information representation, hardware, system software, security, AI, advanced programming, and the practical project. Bridge and enrichment materials included.

18 documents
🌍
IB Diploma Programme

IB DP Computer Science

SL and HL pathways across two years: system fundamentals, computer organisation, networks, HL extensions, IA support, and TOK connections. Full bilingual glossary.

22 documents
📱
ICT Grades 2 – IGCSE

ICT Programme

Progressive digital literacy from primary through IGCSE ICT 0417. Covers productivity tools, spreadsheets, databases, web authoring, cybersecurity, and career pathways.
