Why This Curriculum

Computer Science isn't just another subject on a timetable. It's the language of the world students will graduate into. China is investing in it. Universities are demanding it. The jobs are waiting for it.

I've spent months building this curriculum — not because anyone asked me to, but because I believe every school deserves something exceptional. Every lesson plan, every worksheet, every bilingual glossary: it's all ready.

Built for international schools with EAL learners:

  • Bilingual scaffolding (EN + 中文 + Pinyin) in every document
  • Bronze/Silver/Gold differentiation in every lesson
  • Full mock exam series with gap analysis
  • Standards aligned: Common Core, CIE 0478/0417/9618, IB DP

IGCSE Computer Science

Theory foundations and programming fundamentals. Two-year programme with Paper 1 (Theory) and Paper 2 (Problem Solving & Programming).

  • 5 mock exams with gap analysis
